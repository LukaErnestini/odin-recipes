Tiramisu

Description:

Tiramisu is a sweet dessert originating from Italy. Made with mascarpone cheese, coffee and lady fingers (the buscuits, not literal fingers of ladies, common missconception).

Ingredients:

  • Mascarpone cheese 500g
  • Lady Fingers 500g
  • Coffee
  • ...

Steps:

  1. Mix the ingredients to make the cream
  2. Make coffee, let cool and dip lady fingers briefly. Lay them in layers.
  3. Lightly sprinkle coffee on top
  4. Refrigerate and enjoy when cooled!
